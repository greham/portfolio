My name is Sylvain and I'm a french designer living in Berlin. I started building websites when I was really young and never really stopped. I chased growth all the way here.

I've been living in Berlin, Germany, for 5 years now.

I studied Communication Technologies, Political Sciences, then "Digital Media, Strategy and Design" at the CELSA - Paris Sorbonne University, where we learned about game design, digital storytelling, UX, digital economy, business models, user research...

After experiences in a university research center, ecommerce platform and a couple of startups, I currently work at a digital product studio/agency, namely Goodpatch Berlin, where I worked as a Lead UX Designer for future services and onboard systems in the automotive industry and on a VR project for autonomous vehicles prototyping.

As a designer, I think and build digital products, brands and services.

My UX/UI gig usually revolves around workshop facilitation, service blueprints, putting together prototypes, visual design & UI systems/guidelines/whatever...
Design strategy & management wise, I can help build a design team, introduce and educate management to Design Thinking, promote design as a business opportunity all along the value chain.
