Heisann! My name is Sylvain and I'm a french born designer. I started building websites when I was really young and never really stopped. I chased growth all the way here.

I've been living in Berlin, Germany, for 5 years now. As of 2018, I'm ready to relocate to Oslo, Norway (long story, invite me for a coffee and I'll share it).

I studied Communication Technologies, Political Sciences, then "Digital Media, Strategy and Design" at the CELSA - Paris Sorbonne University.

After experiences in a university research center, ecommerce platform and a couple of startups, I currently work at a digital product studio/agency, namely Goodpatch Berlin, and just returned from 6 months embedded at a major automotive brand as a Lead UX Designer for future services and onboard systems.

I speak French, English, German and I'm getting quite handy with Norwegian Bokm√•l.

I'm working on a more detailed version of my portfolio, built around case studies. Meanwhile, do not hesitate getting in touch so we can have a casual chat about my former projects and experiences.

As a designer, I think and build products, brands and services while helping other designers do the same.

My UX/UI gig usually revolves around workshop facilitation, service blueprints, planning and running user research, putting together prototypes, creating branding and UI systems/guidelines/whatever, carefully crafting icons and assets...
Design strategy & management wise, I can help build a design team, foster the growth of design as one's venture core element and solve higher-level business challenges with management teams.

I pride myself in using "product designer" as a job title, as I try to solve any design challenge without jumping to screen-based solutions without reasons.
Thus, I had the chance to work quite early on some cool AR, IoT and urban farming projects. Eventually, I do my best to stay in tune with the broader business topics in our line(s) of work (digital marketing, VCs, entrepreneurship...)

You may find me experimenting with Radio4000, Hugo and Elias, and doing some freelance work here and there.

On the personal side, I'm a hobby cook, clueless carpenter, leisure sailor and bedroom musician.
