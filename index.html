WorkwiseAbout me

My name is Sylvain and I'm a french born designer. I currently resides in Berlin, Germany, but I'm very interested in relocating to Oslo, Norway (long story).

I am currently working on a newer version to focus more on the UX side of design, where I'd be able to details the project's setup for each product or service. Meanwhile, do not hesitate to get in touch in order to ask for additional samples of design work.

As a designer, I build digital product, brands and services and likes very much helping other designers do a great job.

I mainly worked within the fashion, media, automobile and travel industries, through experiences spanning university research center, agencies, ecommerce platforms and startups.
I also had the chance to work on some cool AR, VR, IoT and urban farming projects.

I currently work at a digital product studio/agency, namely Goodpatch Berlin, where I mainly focus on interaction design, design strategy and design management.

I started building websites when I was really young and never really stopped.
I chased growth all the way here.

I studied Communication Technologies, Political Sciences/Sociology, then Digital Media, Products and Design at CELSA - Paris Sorbonne.

I speak French, English, German and I'm getting quite handy with Norwegian Bokmal.

I am currently learning Unity3D and Cinema4D to be able to have a greater impact on AR and VR projects. I also have my eyes on the Kaospilot trainings in Copenhagen.

You may find me experimenting with Radio4000, Hugo and Elias, and doing some freelance work here and there.

Eventually, I'm a hobby cook, clueless carpenter, leisure sailor and bedroom musician.
