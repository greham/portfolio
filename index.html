Hello human. I work as an experienced UI/UX designer at Zalando SE in Berlin. I like interaction design, product management and user research. I would like to do more HUD, software design, control interfaces for music or data, among other things that you will find out by chatting with me.

I studied Communication Technologies, then jumped to Political Sciences/Sociology, then finally to Digital Media, Marketing and Communication Strategy at Celsa - Paris Sorbonne.

I'm experimenting with Radio4000 and John Voisin, and doing some pro bono for Give Something Back To Berlin.

Most importantly, I'm a hobby cook and carpenter, wannabe sailor, leisure musician soon to be world famous and I always have been in love with the Internet.
