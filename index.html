BIOTHEN WHAT?

Hello friend.

I work as an product designer at Zalando SE in Berlin.
I like interaction design, emerging techs and user research.
For now I'm mainly working on screens, computers and mobiles.

"Screens? Where we’re going, we don’t need screens!" said someone smart.

That's right, this is why I'm really into voice recognition, augmented reality, urban farming and designing social innovation.

Acts are better than declarations, so I'm currently part of exploratory projects for AR and VR at Zalando as their interaction designer, and wrapping my head around some IoT/urban farming thingy with some friends.

I studied Communication and Marketing Technologies, Political Sciences/Sociology, then Digital Media, Products and Design at Celsa - Paris Sorbonne.

Also, it's 2017 and I believe that a product designer has to be marketing and business savvy too. I think this becomes especially true for tomorrow's design/digital product studios, so they can focus on putting work of the highest possible quality and standard into the world, create their own ventures and products... So if you can relate with that, do not hesitate to get in touch.

Eventually, I'm experimenting with Radio4000, Hugurp and John Voisin, and doing some freelance work here and there.

Aside being a designer, I'm also a hobby cook, clueless carpenter, wannabe sailor, leisure musician and bedroom gardener.

Learn to run user research by reading real sociology methodology and behavorial economics.


It's 2017, a product designer is almost a product manager.


Learn Framer.js for work and React.js for fun.


Order your sharpies and post-its by the kilo.


Photoshop is forbidden for anything else than drawing and retouching photographies.


And my monthly piece of advice:

Never install Sketch's updates right away. Always wait a solid week and go check Bohemian Coding issues page on github.
